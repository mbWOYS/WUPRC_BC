We are Western Ukrainian Pipe & Rail Company

WUPRC, Ltd. is a Ukrainian company that deals with steel products since 1999. Having gained solid experience in trading inside Ukraine and exporting to different countries and markets such as England, Germany, India, Vietnam, USA and Canada. We would like to promote our products to customers in North America. We can supply wide range of steel pipes – ERW pipe, SSAW pipe, LSAW pipe, SMLS pipe – dimensions and specifications per your request.

Moreover, we can offer for your demand new pipes of American, German, European, British and Japanese standards of Chinese origin as well as surplus and used shot- blasted pipes of Ukrainian and Russian origin .So if you are interested, feel free to send us your inquiries with accurate demands as for pipes dimensions, standards, quality, quantity, origin and destination point and we will quote FOB/CIF price for you.

Our company offers wide variety of used pipes of Ukrainian and Russian origin. The pipes is after gas and oil products (gasoline, diesel) use. The diameter range is from 9" up to 56" with various wall thickness. The quantities can be very large up to 10000-15000 tons of each diameter. Geographicaly they located over all Russia and Ukraine. We can collect them at the sea port Novorossiysk or Odessa and ship them to USA or worldwide. If you show some interest we can discuss the prices.
